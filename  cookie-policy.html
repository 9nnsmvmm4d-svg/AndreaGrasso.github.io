Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how this website may use cookies and similar technologies, what they are for, and how you can manage your preferences.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website function correctly, remember preferences, and provide information about how the site is used.

Why cookies may be used

This website may use cookies or similar technologies for purposes such as:

  • Essential site functionality.
  • Remembering settings or user preferences.
  • Understanding how visitors use the site.
  • Improving performance and user experience.

Types of cookies that may be used

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Needed for core site functions.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Preference cookies: Used to remember settings where relevant.
  • Third-party cookies: May be set by embedded services or external tools if they are added to the site.

Consent

ICO guidance says cookie information should be clearly available and that non-essential cookies should not simply be hidden inside a privacy policy. It also says cookie information should be easy to find and understand. [web:154][web:168]

If non-essential cookies such as analytics, marketing, or embedded third-party tracking are used, a cookie banner or consent tool should be implemented so visitors can choose whether to accept them. UK-focused compliance guidance also recommends allowing people to change or withdraw consent later. [web:171][web:170]

Managing cookies

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings, including deleting existing cookies or blocking future ones. If a cookie banner is added to this website, you may also be able to manage preferences there.

Third-party services

If this website uses external services such as analytics, embedded media, social media content, or forms, those providers may set their own cookies subject to their own policies.

Changes to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated if the website changes or if new tools and technologies are introduced.
